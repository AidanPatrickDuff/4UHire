About Us



Welcome to 4UHire, your leading car hire outlet in the Dublin area. Here at 4UHire, we value the personal approach. Our company was founded 1952 and since that time we have taken pride in hiring our customers with an impressive range of vehicles at highly competitive rates. As well as regular car categories; Family, Luxury and Automatic, here at 4U Hire we specialize in the provision of vehicles for entire events. For weddings 4UHire provide chauffer driven vehicles for the bridal party, mini busses/busses for wedding guests and discounted car hire for visiting guests.



You should find our booking site very easy to use: feel free to browse our vehicles or events and if you would like to make a booking, you can register an account with us to choose your vehicles and/or events and specify any other requirements you have.



We look forward to hearing from you!
